BACK






Highkey is an app that allows people to easily stay up to date on what's happening in their campus area as well as compete in daily challenges. I joined this project recently and currently work as the lead designer.






MOBILE




The Map

Users can create and follow different spots on the map. If a user is within a close radius to the spot, they can post or comment to the KeyStory.


The Daily Challenges

Users can complete daily challenges submitted by the Highkey team, as well as challenge their videos, and submit new ideas in order to earn a higher key status.
